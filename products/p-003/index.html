激光焊接系统(Laser Welding Systems)

 

用于回流焊、热压焊接和热铆合的控制设备

闭环式温度与时间控制

可存储63个加热曲线

可通过RS-232/485进行远程设置

用于加工控制和辅助监控的两只热电偶

全面而信息丰富的用户界面

可在配搭内置变压器的标准设备及配搭小型控制单元和独立远程变压器的远程设备之间选择

全新Uniflow4可针对柔性电路、带状电缆、电线、SMT元件、单层或双层边缘连接器的焊接以及金质带状连接器的热压帮定提供定向加热与精确温度控制。

新版Uniflow4回流焊控制器进行了重大更新，在多个方面升级了硬件，并加入了过程控制功能和丰富灵活的选项。

Uniflow4可以配备内置变压器的标准回流焊设备形式提供，也可以配备小型控制单元和独立远程变压器的远程设备形式提供（Uniflow4R），其中后者是多种自动化安装应用的理想选择。

Control for reflow soldering, hot bar bonding and heatstaking

Closed-loop temperature and time control

Stores 63 heating profiles

Remote programming via RS-232/485

Two thermocouples for process control and auxiliary monitoring

Comprehensive and informative user interface

Choice of a standard unit with built-in transformer or remote unit with small control unit and remote transformer

The all-new Uniflow4 provides targeted heating and precision temperature control for reflow soldering applications such as the connection of flexible circuits, ribbon cables, wires, SMT components, single or dual sided edge connectors and thermocompression bonding of gold ribbon.

The updated Uniflow4 reflow soldering control is a major update, with a variety of new hardware improvements, process control features, and flexible options.

Uniflow4 is available as a standard reflow soldering unit with a built-in transformer, or as a remote unit (Uniflow4R) with a smaller control unit and separate remote transformer– an ideal option for many automation installations.
